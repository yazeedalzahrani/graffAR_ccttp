WHAT IS GraffAR?

GraffAr is a groundbreaking augmented reality (AR) app designed to revolutionise the way we perceive and interact with graffiti art. Instead of seeing graffiti as vandalism, GraffAr empowers users to transform these urban canvases into platforms for positive expression and creativity.



The impact of GraffAR

01 scover Artistic Potential

Explore your surroundings and uncover hidden artworks waiting to be transformed

02 ugmented Reality Editing

Utilise cutting-edge AR technology to overlay positive messages, vibrant artwork, and inspiring designs onto existing graffiti.

03 Combat Vandalism

Redirect the focus from defacement to enhancement, discouraging further vandalism while promoting a culture of respect and creativity

Join us in revolutionizing the urban landscape with GraffAr. Together, let's turn graffiti into a canvas for positive change
